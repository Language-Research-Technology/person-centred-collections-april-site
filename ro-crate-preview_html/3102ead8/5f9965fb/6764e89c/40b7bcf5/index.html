Angenty waterhole

Angenty is a special waterhole in Anmatyerr and Warlpiri country in Central Australia. The Mer Angenty-warn alhem project team recorded the stories and songs connected to this place. Everyone camped in the riverbed and the elders told stories about the ancestral spirits of the country, including mern amar (mulga apple), arrkerr (barn owl), arnamarwengk (spirit women), tywerrk (native fig), and alharrenty (the monster). Senior women painted up the young girls and taught them some songs and dances.
